Our sponsors

Iremas

It is one of the leading industrial maintenance and engineering solutions companies in Lithuania, providing high-quality services to the energy, industrial, and infrastructure sectors. The company specializes in mechanical equipment installation, electrical engineering, automation, and industrial facility maintenance. With over four decades of experience, Iremas successfully carries out both local and international projects, ensuring safety, reliability, and high work quality. A wide range of partners use the company's services – from the energy and chemical industries to transport infrastructure and manufacturing companies.

Ansys

It is a global leader in engineering simulation software, offering advanced solutions for product development, testing, and optimization. The company's software allows for modeling fluid dynamics, structures, electromagnetics, heat, and multiphysics processes. Ansys solutions are used by various industries – from automotive and aerospace to energy and healthcare.

RapidHarness

It is an advanced wiring design software developed to automate and streamline the entire electrical system design process – from the first schematic to production-ready documentation. This CAD platform enables real-time creation of professional schematics, automated documentation generation, and efficient collaboration within teams or with external partners. RapidHarness solutions are used by engineers across various industries – from automotive and aerospace to electronics and manufacturing.

SolidWorks

It is a global leader in 3D design and engineering solutions, developing advanced software for product development, engineering, and manufacturing. The company specializes in computer-aided design (CAD), analysis (CAE), and product data management (PDM) systems, helping businesses create innovative, efficient, and sustainable solutions. SolidWorks solutions are used across various sectors – from automotive and aerospace to medical devices, electronics, and educational institutions worldwide. The company's mission is to provide engineers with tools that foster creativity, streamline development processes, and accelerate time-to-market.
