About Us

We are an ambitious student team from Lithuania, developing a race car for the Formula Student competition. Our activities cover the entire development process – from team formation, design, and prototyping to manufacturing and preparation for international competitions.

We focus on a combustion-engine-powered car but are already laying the groundwork for future electric vehicles. Our goal is not only to build a competitive race car but also to give students the opportunity to apply their knowledge in engineering, business, and project management in practice.

Mission

We nurture future leaders in the fields of engineering, technology, and business by creating an environment where theoretical knowledge transforms into real-world solutions. As a united team, we not only build a car but also foster a sustainable learning climate that enhances communication and problem-solving skills.

We are committed to promoting growth, building strong partnerships with industry professionals, and pushing the boundaries of student motorsport, contributing to innovation both in Lithuania and internationally.

Competitions

Formula Student is one of the most prestigious engineering competitions in the world, bringing together university engineering students from around the globe who aim to showcase their skills in design, manufacturing, testing, and project management.

In the competition, teams are evaluated not only on the track but also by presenting a business plan, marketing strategies, manufacturing capabilities, and cost analysis. Success depends on creativity, technical expertise, innovation, and teamwork.
